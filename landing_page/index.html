Frequently Asked Questions

What is DeFi?

DeFi, or Decentralized Finance, refers to financial services built on blockchain technology. It allows for peer-to-peer transactions, lending, and borrowing without traditional intermediaries.

Is EclipseFin secure?

Yes, EclipseFin prioritizes security. We utilize blockchain technology and smart contracts to ensure that your assets and transactions are secure.

How do I get started?

To get started, connect your Web3 wallet (e.g., MetaMask, Trust Wallet) to the EclipseFin platform. Then, you can begin trading or participating in yield farming.
